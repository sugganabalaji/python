				===============================================
						Inner OR Nested Loops
				===============================================
=>The Process of Defining One Loop inside another Loop is called Inner Loop OR Nested Loop.
=>The Execution process of Inner loop is that "For Every Value of Outer Loop, Inner Loop Repeated for Finite Number of Times Until Inner Loop Test Becomes False".
=>We can use Inner loops in 4 Approaches. They are

			1. for loop in for loop
			2. while loop in while loop
			3. for loop in while loop.
			4. while loop in for loop
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
